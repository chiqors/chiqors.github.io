Process of creating

As you know, I am going to tell about how do i develop it. But, these 5 steps are the most important keys. I will describe it as best as i can.

  • 01. Research
  • 02. Design
  • 03. Development
  • 04. Testing
  • 05. Deployment

This part is the core of development? Of course, This is where you begins to develop your application from ground up. You have to think about what kind of system are you building for your website. It doesn't just put everything you want in there. But, you also have to think about how the data is flowing in system. Don't worry, I can help you for building this, if you wanted. But, you also need to think about the purpose of your website.


Designing is the process of converting users' needs into a suitable form, which helps me in software coding and implementation. This part is the second step after you have already done your homework for the main system. You can try design your own UI in any forms (Skretch or Computer App Designing). This part, we will have to accept every design, we did. And then, review it with me. Before jumping to the development


Development is the process of designing, programming, documenting of your application. This part, I fully take the charge, since this is why you need me to build your own dream website. Every progress, i have made.. You will be able to see through the Online Board Management, like trello. But, hey.. You can also give your feedback during the development, but it will take longer to finish, only if you want that request in the upcoming version.


Testing is an investigation conducted to provide us with information about the quality of the software product or service under test. It can also provide an objective, independent view of the software to allow us to appreciate and understand the risks of software implementation. My test techniques include the process of executing a program or application with the intent of finding software bugs (errors or other defects), and verifying that the software product is fit for use.


Deployment includes all the process required for preparing our website to run and operate in a specific environment. It involves installation, configuration, testing and making changes to optimize the performance of the software. This is where the last thing in development for me and you before publishing to public. You can help me setup/provide the website hosting or i will try to find a website hosting for you. But, this is not the end, If we want to have this for long term development. We will have to make a new deal on later time.
